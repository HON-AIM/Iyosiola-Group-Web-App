About E-Adem

Our Journey

Our Story

E-Adem Microfinance Company was founded in 2010 with a mission to provide accessible financial services to underserved communities in Nigeria. What started as a small initiative with just five employees has grown into a trusted institution serving over 10,000 clients across multiple states.

Our journey began when our founder, recognizing the challenges faced by small business owners and entrepreneurs in accessing capital, decided to create a solution that combined financial expertise with deep community understanding.

Our Commitment

Financial Inclusion

At E-Adem, we recognize that financial exclusion remains a significant barrier to economic development in Nigeria. Our commitment to financial inclusion drives everything we do:

  • Designing loan products accessible to those without traditional collateral
  • Providing financial literacy training to build long-term capability
  • Offering savings products that help clients build assets securely
  • Expanding our reach to rural and underserved urban communities
  • Partnering with local organizations for greater impact
