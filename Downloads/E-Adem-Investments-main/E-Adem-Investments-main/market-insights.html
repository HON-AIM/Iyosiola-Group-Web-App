The Big Picture

The Microfinance Landscape in Nigeria

Understanding the industry that drives financial inclusion

Overview

The microfinance sector in Nigeria plays a crucial role in financial inclusion, serving millions of individuals and small businesses excluded from traditional banking. With over 800 licensed microfinance institutions, the sector manages assets exceeding ₦1.5 trillion and serves more than 30 million clients nationwide.

Challenges and Opportunities

Despite its growth, the sector faces several challenges:

High operational costs in rural areas

Regulatory compliance complexities

Technology adoption barriers

Client financial literacy gaps

However, these challenges present significant opportunities for innovative institutions like E-Adem to differentiate through technology, financial education, and community-focused models.
